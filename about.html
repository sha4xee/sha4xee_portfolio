My story

My name is Shafi'i Muhammad Waziri, and I'm just getting started in web development. It began by wondering how the websites I used every day were actually made. That small question turned into late nights of tutorials, broken layouts, and the quiet joy of finally getting something to work.

Since then I've been teaching myself, mostly through free resources like freeCodeCamp and a lot of hands-on practice. I recently earned my freeCodeCamp Responsive Web Design certification, and I'm still very much at the beginning of my journey.

In fact, this website is my first real project I built every page of it by hand while learning HTML and CSS. When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading and sketching interface ideas. I like keeping things simple, honest and useful.
